SWAGAT CHA" PEOPLE... CULTURE... TRADITIONS

Uttarakhand, or "Devbhoomi" (Land of the Gods), possesses rich traditions deeply rooted in Himalayan nature and Hindu mythology. Key customs include honoring ancestors through Jagar rituals, performing Aipan, and celebrating nature-centric festivals like Harela and Phool Dei. Community life is defined by folk music, dances (Pandav Nritya), and the, Nath (nose ring) worn by women

NAINI LAKE

Our team will ensure that your trip to Nainital is a joyous travel. The Naini Lake is one of the biggest lakes in India and the most buzzing ones complemented by the Mall Road which hosts a huge number of shopping, eateries and souvenir arena.

KAINCHI DHAM TEMPLE

One of the most famous Temple in Nainital surrounded by the calm greenery, is a must do while you are in Nainital. This Temple is visited by celebrities, masses and spiritual leaders.

ROOF-TOP DECK

A breath taking view of the Nainital Lake with Under the Sky - Rooftop settings, has become quite a favourite space for our guests. Enjoy your elaborate meals, light bites or simply your favourite beverage as you gaze the Nainital Lake for hours.

NAINA DEVI TEMPLE

The much reknowned - Naina Devi temple situated at the banks of the Nainital Lake is only a few steps away from the resort. It is believed that Nainital has got its name after the Naina Devi Temple, a must visit while you are here

THE MALL ROAD

Be it shopping or just a stroll with your loved ones, friends or family, The Mall Road, a colonial designed lakeside pathway is one of the highlights in Nainital. The serenity and buzzing ambiance here will uplift your mind and soul.
