LET'S DINE

Whether you are dining outdoors, at Sizzlers set amidst the mango trees or at the restaurant, you will relish a hearty meal made with the fresh ingredients as the green views increase your appetite. The restaurant, set in the middle of the resort features all stone walls and thatched roof that adds a rustic charm to your stay. The spread put up by our chefs at this restaurant reflect the mood of the markets and with a focus on freshness, the menu features cuisines from across the world. With the calming sight of our well-manicured gardens and the beautiful birds hopping on the trees, your dining experience turns into a pure bliss.

CLASSIC EXPERIENCES

In a peaceful environment, amidst beautiful views there are many classical experiences to make the most of your stay. Take a dip in the pool and rejuvenate at the spa or head out for scenic nature treks, paragliding, rock climbing or kayaking. Kids will love the on-site fun zone and the various indoor activities like table tennis, badminton and karaoke. With a mini forest like landscaping, Country Inn Resort, Jim Corbett is your perfect base to explore the Jim Corbett National Park. The views here are so spellbinding that you are sure to be stunned!

JUNGLE ADVENTURE

Our team will ensure that your trip to Jim Corbett is a joyous adventure. Whether you indulge in bird watching at the resort, head for an Elephant Joy Ride or the Jungle Safari, you will experience the true essence of the jungle. You can also plan to laze in your Tree Top Cottage or take leisurely walks in the resort’s landscaped gardens, reminiscing a mini jungle.

PALMS, THE SPA

After a long day of sightseeing in Jim Corbett, head to the spa to choose from an array of therapies that will leave you recharged for another adventure. With the blend of modern techniques and nature-inspired ingredients, our expert therapists will make you feel completely rejuvenated.

EXPERIENTIAL DINING

Dining at Country Inn Resort, Jim Corbett turns experiential as you are surrounded by the chirpy birds and beautiful green views. Our garden restaurant ‘Sizzlers’, set amidst the tall mango trees and overlooking the swimming pool serve sizzlers from across the world. The signature starry night dinner with backdrop of the waterfall, made magical with the live music is an experience our guests take along, in their hearts.
